## Stage Definitions

- **New** — Lead came in. Not yet contacted.
- **Contacted** — First reply sent. Awaiting response.
- **Discovery** — On a discovery call; gathering requirements.
- **Demoed** — Showed the demo. Awaiting decision.
- **Negotiating** — Discussing price, scope, or terms.
- **Pending payment** — Invoice sent.
- **In delivery** — Paid 50%, code in their repo, deployment scheduled.
- **Live** — Deployed and accepted. In support window.
- **Closed (won)** — Final invoice paid.
- **Closed (lost)** — Did not buy. Reason captured in notes.
